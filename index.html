About EXPRESS DELIVERY SERVICES

Welcome to EXPRESS DELIVERY SERVICES, your trusted partner in global logistics. With over a decade of experience, we specialise in delivering packages swiftly and securely to your doorstep. Our state-of-the-art tracking system allows you to monitor your shipments in real-time, while our dedicated team ensures every package is handled with care. From ground transportation to air freight, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet your needs. Our headquarters in New York serves as the hub of our operations, supported by a network spanning multiple continents.

Our commitment to sustainability includes eco-friendly packaging and optimised delivery routes to reduce our carbon footprint. Whether you're a business sending bulk orders or an individual shipping a personal item, we provide personalised solutions with competitive rates. Join thousands of satisfied customers who rely on us for their delivery needs, and experience the difference of a service that prioritises speed, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

About Us

EXPRESS DELIVERY SERVICES was founded in 2015 with a mission to revolutionise global logistics. Based in New York, we pride ourselves on our commitment to speed, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our team of over 500 professionals operates a vast network across North America, Europe, and Asia, ensuring your packages reach their destination on time. We leverage cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices to provide world-class delivery solutions for businesses and individuals alike.

Our mission is to connect the world through efficient and eco-friendly delivery services. With a focus on innovation, we continuously improve our processes to meet the evolving needs of our customers. Join us on our journey to make delivery faster, greener, and more reliable.
